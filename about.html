About StellarFeed

StellarFeed is a web application designed to bring you a daily collection of cosmic content. We aim to provide an engaging and visually appealing experience for anyone interested in astronomy and space exploration. Our goal is to make the universe a little more accessible to everyone.

API Integrations

This application is powered by data from two fantastic third-party APIs:

  • **NASA Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD):** Used to fetch the daily featured space image and its explanation.
  • **NewsAPI.org:** Used to provide the latest news articles related to space and astronomy.
